Fairbanks, AK Commuter Rail

I had a bit of fun imagining a commuter rail service for the city of Fairbanks, AK (with minimal infrastructure changes, but still enough to get a bit more than 1 train every 2 hours).
Regular service through Fairbanks, AK from Ester, AK to North Pole, AK. Weekly flag stop service to Nenana.
Stations (West to East) : Nenana, North Nenana, Manley, Dunbar, Standard, Saulich, Ester, University of Alaska, Fairbanks, Badger, North Pole & Moose Creek.
